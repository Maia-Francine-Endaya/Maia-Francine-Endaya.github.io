SaluSalo: A VR Filipino Cooking Sim

Come experience the world of Filipino Cuisine!

Let There Be Light

Bring light back into the world

Let There Be Light is a puzzle-platformer game where you must bring light back to the world by ascending the tower with the orb. This is the project that allowed me to explore pixel art. It became the starting point for my own pixel art journey. This project also made me discover the process of level design.
